NCPC Team Naming Conventions

This document describe the conventions used to name teams in NCPC. In order for ranklists to be easily merged and filtered by specifc countries/universities, it is important that these conventions are followed.

A team name should be either "Team Name(<University>/<Country>)" or "Team Name(open:<Site>/<Country>)", depending on whether the team is in the open class or not. Here,

  • "<University>" is the university of the team (see list below).
  • "<Country>" is one of "no", "se", "is", "dk" or "fi".
  • "<Site>" is the university where the open team is located (again, see list below).
