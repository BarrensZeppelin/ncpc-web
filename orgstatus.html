NCPC 2006 status

Problem set schedule

1. May All ideas for problems are presented before this date. Views on problems are presented and discussed in the following period.
1. June The final problems are selected.
1. August Problems and test data are finished by their respective authors. Do not postpone this until the last minute if you do not have to!
1. September Deadline for comments and requests for improvements in problem definitions and test data authored by others.
10. September Final deadline for changes in problems. Any changes after this date must be approved by the head judge.
30. September Competition is held.
